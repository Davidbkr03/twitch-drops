How to get your auth token
  1. Log into twitch.tv on your normal browser
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools → Application tab
  3. Under Cookies → https://www.twitch.tv, find auth-token
  4. Copy the Value and paste it above

The auth token lets the bot use your Twitch session directly — no login page needed. Tokens persist across restarts.
